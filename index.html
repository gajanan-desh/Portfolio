Identity governance meets operational reliability

I specialize in Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) using SailPoint IdentityIQ — developing workflows, rules, and custom connectors that govern the full identity lifecycle for enterprise banking clients. My work spans application onboarding, provisioning automation, access certification, and integrations across SCIM, LDAP, Active Directory, JDBC, and REST/SOAP web services.

Alongside IAM, I maintain the infrastructure that keeps identity platforms running: CI/CD pipelines in Jenkins and Bitbucket that automate SailPoint deployments across Dev, UAT, and Production, containerized workflows with Docker and Kubernetes, and cloud infrastructure on AWS. I built Grafana dashboards to monitor platform and server health — closing the loop between provisioning logic and the systems it runs on.

I'm known for careful troubleshooting, close collaboration with application teams during onboarding, and documentation that makes handoffs painless.
